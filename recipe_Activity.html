Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 50g grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic (optional)
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente.
  2. Fry pancetta until crisp. Add garlic if using, then remove it after flavoring the oil.
  3. In a bowl, mix with Parmesan cheese.
  4. Drain pasta and combine with pancetta.
  5. Season with pepper and serve immediately.
